Prussian blue palette

Slightly different, more cyan-tinted blue colors for Doom. These shades have appeared in various total conversions, often alongside altered red tones, but in this PWAD only the blue colors have been changed.

I honestly don't know who originally created this color set, but hopefully it won't be lost again!

Download

Archive with palette:

  •   bluepal.zip

To use, load the game with:

doom.exe -file bluepal.wad

…or drop it into any suitable autoload folder.
